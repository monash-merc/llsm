net.imglib2.realtransform

RealShearTransform 

class RealShearTransform extends InvertibleRealTransform with BoundingBoxTransform

Invertible shear transform that is performed in real space. coordinate[shearDimension] += coordinate[referenceDimension] * shearFactor

Inverse is calculated by taking the inverse of the shearFactor.
